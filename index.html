History

Drumcode Records is a Swedish techno record label run by Adam Beyer. Since it was created in 1996, the label has released several hits, unveiled new talents, and participated in radio and in conjunction with other international labels. In 2017-2018, Drumcode was the best-selling techno label on Beatport, according to BeatStats, and the third best-selling label on Beatport of all time. Although Drumcode originally only worked with Swedish or Scandinavian producers, it now also works with DJs of other nationalities whose sound fits the musical lineup.
